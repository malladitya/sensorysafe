4. **Show, don't tell**: Live demo is more powerful than slides

7. **Emphasize impact**: Focus on how it helps people

## ‚ùì ANTICIPATED QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

**Q: How accurate is the AI prediction?**
A: Currently 70-80% based on time and community data. With ML, we'll reach 90%+ accuracy.

**Q: How do you prevent fake reports?**
A: GPS verification, rate limiting, and future user reputation system.

**Q: What about privacy?**
A: No personal data stored. Reports are anonymous with only location/timestamp.

**Q: How is this different from Waze?**
A: Waze focuses on traffic. We focus on sensory comfort - noise, crowds, lighting.

**Q: Can it work offline?**
A: Not yet, but it's on our roadmap as a Progressive Web App.

**Q: What's your business model?**
A: Freemium - free for individuals, premium for organizations (schools, therapy centers).

**Q: How will you scale?**
A: Cloud infrastructure (Azure/AWS), database integration, CDN for global reach.

**Q: What about other disabilities?**
A: Our accessible design helps everyone. Future: customizable for different needs.
